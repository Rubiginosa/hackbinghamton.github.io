HackBU Schedule

Our main recurring event is our weekly workshop, which is held every Monday at 7:30PM. We also have occasional special events on other days, sometimes in collaboration with other Computer Science groups on campus.

We announce our events a day before on our mailing list, and the day of on our Discord server.
